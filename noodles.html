Relationships Between Noodles

This is a network of different types of Chinese noodles and their relationships to each other. The noodles visualization illustrates how Chinese noodles are related from two common regions: Southern and Northern. The nodes represent different noodles types.

 The raw data in csv format can be downloaded from here. We have used the webweb to visualize the network.
