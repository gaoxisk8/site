Project Info:

Often inspired by the natural and urban landscapes I lived in, my work explores the relationships between the environment and myself. Stitching-hit color design and elaborate textures are the principal elements of my pursuit.

I desire to create sober designs that lengthen its life cycle and cherish the value of traditional craftsmanship, both of which resist consumerism.
